About Us

Zen Apps LLC is an independent iOS app studio based in the United States. We design and publish apps exclusively on the Apple App Store, with a sharp focus on user experience and purposeful simplicity.

Every product we ship is crafted to eliminate friction and do one thing exceptionally well. We believe the best software feels effortless — intuitive from the first launch, reliable in daily use.

Beyond publishing our own apps, we offer software development services to clients who share our commitment to quality. Whether it's a consumer app or a business tool, we bring the same precision and care to every project.
