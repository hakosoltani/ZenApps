Privacy Policy

Effective: March 4, 2026 Company: Zen Apps LLC
Zen Apps LLC ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This policy describes what data our apps collect, how we use it, and your rights. We keep it short because our data practices are genuinely simple.

1 Information We Collect

Our apps are designed to work with minimal data. Depending on the specific app, we may collect:

  • Information you provide — such as preferences, settings, or content you enter directly into the app.
  • Usage data — anonymous analytics (e.g., crash reports, feature usage) collected through Apple's built-in frameworks or third-party tools like Firebase or similar, used solely to improve app performance.
  • Device information — iOS version, device model, and app version for diagnostic purposes.
  • Contact information — if you email us for support, we retain your email address to respond to your inquiry.

We do not collect names, payment details, precise location, or any sensitive personal data unless explicitly stated within a specific app's supplemental privacy notice.

2 How We Use Your Information

We use collected data solely to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our applications
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Respond to support requests and user feedback
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3 Data Sharing

We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — trusted third parties (e.g., crash reporting tools) who help us operate our apps, bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Apple — app distribution and payments are handled by Apple under their own privacy policy.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights.

4 Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law. Anonymous analytics data may be retained indefinitely in aggregated form.

5 Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to certain processing activities
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@thezenapps.com.

6 Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have inadvertently collected such information, we will promptly delete it.

7 Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. You use our apps at your own risk.

8 Third-Party Links & Services

Our apps may link to third-party websites or integrate third-party SDKs (e.g., App Store, analytics providers). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those parties. Please review their policies independently.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be noted by updating the effective date above. Continued use of our apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
